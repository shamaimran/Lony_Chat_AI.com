Frequently Asked Questions

What is Lony Chat?

Lony Chat is a free, browser-based AI assistant powered by Google's Gemini 2.5 Flash model. It helps you with conversations, coding, image generation, voice input, and productivity tasks — all while keeping your data completely private.

Is my data safe? Does Lony Chat store my chats?

Yes, 100% safe! All your chats, images, and data are stored only in your browser's local storage. Nothing is sent to any server except when generating responses or images via the Gemini API (which Google processes). We don't collect, store, or share any personal information.

Do I need an API key to use Lony Chat?

Yes. You need a free Google Gemini API key to use advanced features like chat, image generation, and code help. Go to Settings → API Key in the app and paste your key from Google AI Studio.

Is Lony Chat completely free?

The app itself is completely free and open to use. However, you need your own Gemini API key, and Google may charge for heavy usage beyond their free tier.

Can I export or clear my chat history?

Yes! Use the "Export JSON" button to save your chats, and "Import JSON" to restore them. To clear everything, click "Clear All Data" in the Settings menu.

Why does Lony Chat run locally in the browser?

Running locally gives you maximum privacy and speed. No data leaves your device unless you request a response from Gemini. This makes Lony Chat faster and more secure than many cloud-based AI tools.

Will you add more AI models in the future?

Yes! We are continuously improving Lony Chat. Future updates may include support for other models, better voice features, themes, and more tools.
